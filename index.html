The Problem

Human knowledge is locked away.

AI companions are rapidly changing how people learn, ask questions, and engage with ideas. Yet most are designed for entertainment, optimized for engagement, and disconnected from the people whose ideas shaped our world.

From Ibn Khaldun to Noam Chomsky, from Taha Hussein to Azmi Bishara, humanity's most important conversations are becoming harder, not easier to access.

The Solution

We Build Digital Twins of Great Minds

We call them Emulated Identities (EIs) — AI personas that simulate the voice, memory, reasoning style, and intellectual positions of each thinker, powered by a RAG system built on their full personal archive.

How It Works

From Archive to Living Conversation

The EI Architecture
RAG-Powered Knowledge Base
Full archive of books & publications
Decades of TV and radio interviews
Articles, speeches, and unpublished notes
Editorially verified and curated data
Arabic-native LLM backbone
1
Build the archive

We collect and verify every book, interview, article, and speech for the figure — decades of output, carefully curated.

2
Train the Identity (EI)

A RAG system built on the full archive feeds an Arabic-native LLM. The EI learns the thinker's voice, logic, opinions, and intellectual evolution.

3
Verify with the source

For living figures, each EI is reviewed and approved by the person themselves — creating a verified, trust-backed identity, not a hallucination.

4
Deploy across platforms

The EI is accessible via voice on the app, in classrooms, through museum kiosks, or as a live podcast guest — one identity, many surfaces.

Where Dhaqira Comes to Life

Dhaqira Podcast
The Interactive Podcast

Audience members dial in and interview the digital twin live. The conversation becomes publishable content — a podcast where every listener can be the host. Think Shahid Ala Al-Asr, reimagined.

Dhaqira Class
Classroom Learning

Students dialogue with historical thinkers in real time. A World Bank study found 6 weeks of AI-assisted learning equalled 2 years of traditional study.

Dhaqira Library
Research Tool

Search a thinker's full archive by meaning, not just keywords. Find the exact quote, page, and publication — turning rare collections into living research companions.

Dhaqira Hall
Museums & Heritage Sites

Physical kiosks let museum visitors hold conversations with historical figures. Exhibits become interactive. Joe Biden reflects on his presidency in his own words.

Dhaqira App
Daily Companion

Access the full library of historical and contemporary figures from your phone. The first AI companion built for Arab identity, culture, and intellectual tradition.

Built for Trust

Designed for Integrity, Not Imitation

Anyone can prompt an LLM to "act like" a thinker. Dhaqira does something different — building verified, archive-backed identities that are impossible to replicate.

📖
Deep Archive, Not Guesswork

Every EI is trained on the complete archive of the figure: books, interviews, articles, speeches — decades of intellectual output, rigorously curated.

✅
Source-Verified by the Person

Living figures review and approve their EI before it goes live. This isn't hallucination — it's a verified, ongoing collaborative relationship.

🌍
Arabic-Native, Culturally Grounded

Built on Arabic-native models with deep cultural context — not a Western model with Arabic text pasted in. The difference is felt in every conversation.
